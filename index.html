Article I — About

I am a passionate law student from Bangladesh with a strong interest in Constitutional Law, Labour Law, Contract Law, legal research, legal writing, and legal education. I enjoy creating legal notes, presentations, study materials and educational content that make legal topics easier to understand for the people around me.

My long-term goal is to become a skilled legal professional while contributing to society through education, volunteerism and public awareness — treating the law not just as a subject to pass exams in, but as a tool for service.
